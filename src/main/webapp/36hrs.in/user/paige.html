Buenos Aires on a Shoestring

Buenos Aires

Part of the allure of this South American capital city is that you can soak in the delights of European-influenced architecture and culture without the hefty price tag. Museums, events and restaurants can cater to any budget, especially those who must keep an extra eye on where their money goes. Despite the pesky inflation rate, which ensures that your café con leche today will most surely cost more in a few months' time, Buenos Aires remains an attractive destination for those with a backpacker's budget, or mindset.

Girls' Trip to Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires

Even though Buenos Aires makes quite a hard sell when it comes to sultry sexiness and romance, it's actually a great city to enjoy with a group of close friends. Unexpected activities, trendsetting restaurants, and hip shopping districts make it an ideal place for a girls' trip. Besides, wine and ice cream are more fun when shared, right?

Buenos Aires after Sundown

Buenos Aires

A large part of the magic of Buenos Aires is that it truly is a city that never sleeps. Dinners that start at midnight and late-night parties that last until well beyond sunrise mean that getting some shuteye takes a backseat in the porteño list of priorities. If you've come to BA for a good time, it won't be hard to fill the agenda with a variety of parties and social events. The best part? New spots are always opening up, so check with the locals to find out where the party du jour will be popping up.

About Us

We're here to help you discover, collect and share amazing activities, places and things to do in a city. Perhaps you have a bucket list of places you want to visit. Or perhaps you have wonderful memories of a past vacation. Our site lets you catalog and collect your favorite places in a city and share the highlights with your friends. You can also browse our collection of expert itineraries, which are carefully curated by local writers and cater to a variety of interests and budgets.

Please enjoy your visit and safe travels,

The 36hrs Team

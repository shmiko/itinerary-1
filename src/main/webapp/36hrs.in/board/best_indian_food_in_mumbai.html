Best Indian food in Mumbai

Mumbai

 by Ravijot Chugh
foodie

I think my favorite part about Mumbai is the food, in particular the Indian food. I didn't realize how varied Indian cuisine really is until I sampled a few of these places.

Peshawari

Best North Indian food in the city. Sister resraurant of Bukhara - restaurant that consistently features in world's best list from India

Britannia & Co.

Nestled in the lanes of Ballard Estate, Britannia still retains its old world charm. Open only for lunch,the most famous of the iconic Parsi eateries is a Mumbai institution. Chicken Berry Pulao and Sali Boti are mouth watering signature dishes here.

A cozy restaurant specializng in Mangalorean preparations. One of the famed sea food restaurants in Mumbai along with Mahesh. Butter crab and Hyderabadi Fish Tikka are must haves.

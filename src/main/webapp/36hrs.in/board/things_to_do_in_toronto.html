Things to do in Toronto

Toronto

 by Jennifer Blumberg
sightseeing

Toronto has plenty of cool sightseeing. Some of my favorite memories include getting an adrenaline rush on the CN Tower's glass floor, sneaking into the box seats at a Blue Jays game and waiting two hours for a grilled cheese sandwich at Wayne Gretzky's (never going back to THAT place again!)

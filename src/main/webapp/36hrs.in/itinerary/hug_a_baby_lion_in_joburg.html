Hug a baby lion in Joburg

Johannesburg

 by Jennifer Blumberg
$$
sightseeing
outdoors

People point to Cape Town as the most picturesque city in South Africa, and that's probably true. But Cape Town doesn't have baby lions to pet or the world's best weather. Johannesburg (or Joburg or Jozi as the locals call it) is dirty, gritty, complicated and full of hidden treasures. It is, and will always be, one of my favorite places to live and visit.

The City of Kings

Lima

 by Walter Rhein
$$
foodie
sightseeing

Over the last decade Lima has transformed itself from a checkpoint on the way to Machu Picchu into a gastronomic wonderland and world-class tourist destination in its own right. Buoyed by a tremendous economy, recent investments in infrastructure have put Lima on the map to stay. The City of Kings can now boast a series of fun and elegant activities that will delight and entertain any traveler, so extend your South American vacation a few more days and take part in all that Lima has to offer.

This is the flagship restaurant that launched Gaston Acurio's career and also began the gastronomic revolution that has spread throughout all of Peru. Trained in France, Gaston prepares traditional Peruvian plates with various cooking techniques from around the world. This “fusion” method has served as the template for various culinary artists to experiment and expand Peruvian flavors to the fullest. A dining experience at Astrid & Gaston consists of several courses, and can last up to two hours or more. But don't be concerned, despite the world class service and quality, the prices are very reasonable. Don't forget to plan ahead, this restaurant is so popular that it can become fully booked months in advance.

Lima has a healthy night life, but the party doesn't really start until after midnight. In the meantime, there are plenty of trendy bars with social atmospheres where you can enjoy a few casual cocktails before the dancing begins. The O Bar is about a twenty minute walk from Astrid & Gaston and features a wide variety of delicious beverages made with Peruvian Pisco. Try a Pisco Sour, your visit to Peru will not be complete until you do.

Aura
thumbnails
calendar

11:45pm

A trip to South America would not be complete without a night of dancing. Aura stays open until 7 A.M. and you're sure to experience a party there that you won't soon forget. From the O Bar, Aura is approximately a 10 minute taxi ride. Nestled within the Larco Mar shopping area, this is one of the safest disco locations in the whole city.

Costa Verde
thumbnails
calendar

11:00am

Peru has some of the best surfing beaches in the world as world champion Sofia Mulanovich will attest. Whether you're an expert or a novice you can head down to the Costa Verde beach, just below Larco Mar, and rent a wet suit and board for a very affordable price. The locale is very casual as surf instructors arrive and set up a tent with their equipment. All you have to do is approach them and negotiate a price.

You'll be hungry after surfing, but the physical activity often demands a lighter meal. For that reason, I'd recommend Hot & Cool, the small sandwich shop on Calle Berlin. Chef Julio provides a gourmet, economical sandwich in a very casual setting. It may not look like much, but Hot & Cool has been Lonely Planet's choice for best sandwich shop in Peru for the last 3 years

Huaca Pucllana

calendar

The Huaca Pucllana is a giant adobe pyramid which was built by the pre-Inca Wari culture between 200 and 700 AD. This is a spectacular set of ruins located right in the heart of Lima, and not to be missed. It is approximately a fifteen minute walk or five minute cab ride from Hot & Cool.

Peru has some of the best sushi in the world. Edo Sushi is approximately a ten minute walk from Huaca Pucllana. This is one of the smaller Sushi restaurants in Miraflores so there is almost never a need for a reservation. The atmosphere is cordial, the food is excellent, and the prices are quite reasonable.

Parque Kennedy is a medium sized park located just across the street from the Ovalo de Miraflores. On the weekends, artists line the streets with paintings for sale. There is also a small market at the center where people sell hand-crafted items like shawls and earrings. It's the perfect place to combine an evening stroll with some gift shopping.

One of the best activities to do in any country is to simply sit and watch people go by. By far the best location for this in Peru is Cafe Haiti at the Ovalo de Miraflores. The historic cafe is open to the street and you can sit in a small table right next to the foot traffic as you enjoy a number of expertly prepared caffeinated beverages.
